What We Supply

Equipment Categories

From gate valves to gaskets, we stock and source the full range of flow control and piping components your operations require.

Gate Valves

Full-port and reduced-bore gate valves for isolation service in high-pressure pipelines and process systems.

Globe Valves

Precision throttling and flow regulation valves for steam, water, and industrial fluid applications.

Check Valves

Swing, piston, and tilting-disc check valves to prevent backflow in critical systems.

Valve Components

Stems, seats, discs, bonnets, packing, and repair kits to maintain and extend valve service life.

Gaskets & Seals

Spiral wound, ring joint, and sheet gaskets for flange connections across all pressure classes.

Fittings & Adapters

Flanges, couplings, adapters, and specialty fittings for pipe system assembly and modification.
